About Btech Walleh

Btech Walleh is a dynamic platform designed to host and manage online quizzes for students. Our goal is to make learning fun and competitive through well-structured quizzes, live leaderboards, and exciting rewards. From technical quizzes to aptitude challenges, we offer a wide variety of tests to boost your skills and confidence.

Whether you're preparing for placements or want to test your academic knowledge, our quizzes are tailored to challenge and help you grow. Engage with fellow learners, participate in events, and celebrate your victories with certificates and goodies.

Interactive Quizzes

Engage in topic-wise quizzes with instant feedback and scoring. Track progress, revisit questions, and master every concept effectively.

Each quiz is crafted by experts and reviewed regularly to match current academic and industry trends.

Live Leaderboards

Compete in real-time and see where you stand among peers during contests and events.

Leaderboard updates every second, keeping you motivated to push harder and aim for the top spot.

Rewards & Certificates

Community Engagement
